In 2018, I graduated from Gustavus Adolphus College, where I earned a B.A. in Spanish and a B.A. in Fine Arts. Before graduating, I studied Computer Science for a year. Since graduating, I've had the opportunity to explore freelance photography and graphic design alongside a fellow freelancer. Additionally, in my free time I've been expanding on my understanding of OOP, Java and application development.

In addition to design and technology, I'm very interested in the pursuit of efficiency and progress through enterprise systems and technology interactions. I am always going to continue on that path of learning and I strive to do so while collaborating with others in a strong team environment.
